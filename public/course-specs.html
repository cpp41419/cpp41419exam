What is CPP41419?

CPP41419 Certificate IV in Real Estate Practice is Australia's nationally recognized qualification for real estate professionals.

Replacing the superseded CPP40307, this updated qualification meets current industry standards and is required for real estate licensing across all Australian states and territories.

Quick Facts

Course Code: CPP41419
Level: Certificate IV
Duration: 6-12 months

Modules & Curriculum

The CPP41419 course consists of 12 units (8 core + 4 electives) covering all essential real estate competencies:

Core Modules (Mandatory)

Professional Practice

  • • CPPREP4001: Prepare for professional practice
  • • CPPREP4002: Access and interpret ethical practice
  • • CPPREP4003: Access and interpret legislation

Property Fundamentals

  • • CPPREP4004: Establish marketing profiles
  • • CPPREP4005: Work with trust accounts
  • • CPPREP4101: Appraise property

Elective Modules (Choose 4)

Sales Pathway

  • • CPPREP4102: Market property
  • • CPPREP4105: Conduct auctions
  • • CPPREP4123: Identify properties to list

Property Management

  • • CPPREP4121: Establish landlord relationships
  • • CPPREP4122: Manage tenant relationships
  • • CPPREP4103: Appraise residential rentals

Advanced Skills

  • • CPPREP4125: Transact in trust accounts
  • • CPPREP5002: Manage risk
  • • CPPREP4107: Present and negotiate

Typical Course Structure
