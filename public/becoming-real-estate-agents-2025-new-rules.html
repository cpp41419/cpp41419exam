Become a Real Estate Agent in NSW

Your 2025 Step-by-Step Guide

1. Complete Certificate IV

The Certificate IV in Real Estate Practice (CPP41419) forms the educational foundation for all NSW real estate careers.

  • CPPREP4001: Prepare for professional practice
  • CPPREP4002: Access and interpret ethical practice
  • CPPREP4003: Access and interpret legislation

2. Apply for Registration

Once you've completed your CPP41419 training, the next step is obtaining your Certificate of Registration.

Application Process

  1. Complete application through NSW Fair Trading
  2. Submit qualification evidence
  3. Pass mandatory probity checks

3. Gain Experience

The transition from registration to full license requires accumulating practical experience under supervision.

4. Apply for Full License

The transition to full licensing represents the culmination of your qualification journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to become an agent?

The complete pathway typically requires 18-24 months from starting education to full licensing.
